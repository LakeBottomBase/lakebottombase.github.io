Front Matter

It won't be intentional, but this space will probably gain the most content on this site. I am a product of strict religion, and was mentally shaped through both nature and nurture, for many decades, in how I was supposed to think and act, and what my posture was supposed to be towards the world. Shedding those layers alternates somewhere between difficult and impossible, most of the time.

So, yes, I have some thoughts.

"...and ye shall know the truth, and the truth shall make you free."


I've dabbled off and on with blogging over the years, but this is the first time I have real control over the entire site, how it's laid out, and what I want here, so I'm not stuck with simple blog posts of where I'm at, posted in reverse chronological order. This space is much more like a "digital garden" that I can tend with flexibility and freedom, letting it grow over time into whatever it becomes.

That said, this will eventually be a link to some thoughts on religion. Don't take it too seriously. None of it matters nearly as much as I once believed.
